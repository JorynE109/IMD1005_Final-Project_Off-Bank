About Off-Bank

“What is there to do on a Saturday night in Ottawa?”, is a question every 20-something year-old in this city has asked themselves many, many times. As music lovers, we understand the frustrations that come with living in a bureaucrat’s suburb, the main one being where the hell do you find good music and cool people? Turns out, it’s easier than you may think, if you know where to look. Off-Bank is a student-run organization seeking to create a hub for events in the Ottawa alternative music scene. Our goal is to showcase local artists and venues, and offer an easy way to stay up-to-date on events and shows in the Ottawa area.
