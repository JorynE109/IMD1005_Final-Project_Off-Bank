What to Bring to a Concert

Joryn's ultimate guide for concert preparedness.

BEFORE WE BEGIN

There are some important details to get right before you go to a show

  • EAT!! What are you thinking going to a show on an empty stomache?
  • HYDRATE! Having to piss is better than passing out my friend.
  • Charge your damn phone if you need it to get home.

Okay you've taken care of yourself? Now let's get that bag packed

Bring these

If you're anything like me, you'd bleed out before asking venue staff for first aid equipment, so having a basic first aid kit is a good idea:

  • Band-Aids
  • Advil / Acetemetophen

Basic hygene is important:

  • Deodorant

    Not enough of y'all remember this one.. (side note: if you're not gonna need to re-apply then no need to bring it, but pls wear some <3)

  • Eating? Floss is always nice just in case

DO NOT bring these

Does this venue check bags? Do they pat you down before entering? Do you not know the answers to these? If so, please do not bring with you a flask or any outside substances. It's safer for everyone and you really don't want to get kicked out.

This should go without saying but do not bring any weapons or sharp objects.
